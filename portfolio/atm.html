tabula rasa

this mixtape has been a long time coming. i started working on a second album,
called "falling," in late 2019. i spent most of my time the last 5 years trying to perfect
each song for that album, going through cycles of making 10-20 songs i thought could work
before making a song that was "my best yet" and feeling as though i needed to reset.
each new peak came with the thought that "none of those songs can stand up to this one."
and so 10 or 20 more songs would come, before a new peak, and a new cycle.

in november of 2023, in trying to transfer the data on my harddrive to a new, faster drive,
i lost access to the mixes for alot of my old songs. I was tired of restarting.
I knew i had hundreds of songs, and i hadn't released an music in nearly 3 years.
i have always been a bit obsessed with the idea of a cohesive concept album,
one that flows all the way through. this is not that. alot of these songs i don't relate to
anymore, but in taking a step back and looking at all
of the work i have done in the last 5 years of my life, i realized that i did relate to them.
and more important than that, they are a benchmark of my progress.
